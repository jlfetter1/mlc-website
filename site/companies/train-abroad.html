What a program includes

Language training. Cultural immersion. Network.

Every Train Abroad program integrates language training before, during, and after travel. Programs vary in intensity — from light English classes during a corporate trip to a complete immersion semester.

01 · Language Training

Integrated, before / during / after

Pre-trip prep to lock in the foundation. Daily sessions on the ground if the program calls for full immersion. Post-trip reinforcement to consolidate. Calibrated to participant level (CEFR-aligned).

02 · Cultural Integration

Off-campus immersion

Cultural visits, industry tours, guest lectures. English-speaking host families optional, typical for longer programs.

03 · Network

Curated introductions

Local industry peers, alumni, MLC-affiliated executives. A network that outlasts the program.
