Ready-Set-Go · Language Coaching

Five steps. Built around your event.

A repeatable framework that scales from a board presentation to an investor call to a regulatory hearing.

01

Needs Analysis

  • Pre-Coaching Interview and Survey
02

Set: Rehearsal

  • First run-through of the presentation
  • Provide feedback on delivery (intonation, pronunciation, structure, confidence)
  • Provide detailed notes
03

Ready: Preparation and Refinement

  • Review and Practice Final Presentation
  • Refine vocabulary, syntax, and grammar
  • Prepare student for first run-through (online meeting)
04

Be: Final Run

  • Final 'mock' run-through of the complete presentation
  • Focus on pacing, timing, and correction of recurrent stress
  • Provide detailed final feedback and recording of final run-through
05

Post-Coaching

  • Brief Survey and Feedback Session
