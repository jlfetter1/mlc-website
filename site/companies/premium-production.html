The problem with most executive English programs

Your team doesn't need more vocabulary.
They need more reps.

Most language programs are 70% passive — listen, read, fill in the blanks. Premium Production inverts that. 80% of every session is production: your executives running a pitch, handling a cross-examination, closing a negotiation. The instructor intervenes only to refine, never to lecture. Fluency isn't knowledge — it's muscle. We build the muscle.
