Why a certification program is its own product

It's not teaching language.
It's teaching the exam.

A CEFR C1 speaker can still fail IELTS if they don't know the format. Accreditation prep inverts the usual order: we start with the exam's scoring rubric and work backward. Your candidate knows exactly how the graders evaluate every task, what the time boundaries are, what the traps are, and what the model answers look like. Language fluency is the foundation; exam craft is what gets the score.
