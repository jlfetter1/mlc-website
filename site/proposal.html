What you're getting

Two systems that work together. The website attracts and impresses your corporate clients. The platform manages every aspect of running the school once they sign up.

MLC Website

A premium marketing site that positions MLC as the top-tier choice for executive language training. Animated, bilingual (English/Spanish), and designed to impress C-suite decision-makers. Live on the web, updates automatically when you push changes.

02

The Website

A high-end B2B site that competes visually with companies like Stripe and Apple. It shows your 8 service lines, client logos (BMW, PepsiCo, TV Azteca, Grupo Salinas), testimonials, and a clear path to get in touch.

  • Animated & interactive — smooth scroll effects, glowing backgrounds, and subtle motion that makes the site feel alive without being distracting
  • Fully bilingual — every word on the site works in English and Spanish, toggled with one click
  • Mobile-ready — looks great on phones, tablets, and desktops
  • Fast — no heavy frameworks, loads instantly
  • Auto-deploys — changes go live within minutes of being approved
03

The Platform

Four separate dashboards, each designed for the person using it:

Admin Dashboard

Your operations team sees everything: a calendar of all classes, student enrollment pipeline, teacher management, company accounts, and system-wide analytics. Includes an AI chat assistant that can answer questions about your data instantly ("How many students does BMW have?" or "Which groups have low attendance?").

Teacher Dashboard

Teachers see today's classes, click to join Zoom, mark attendance after each session, and enter grades at the end of each period. They also set their weekly availability so admin knows who's free when.

Student Dashboard

Students log in to see their upcoming classes, click to join Zoom, review their attendance history and grades, and manage their account details. Gives them a single place to stay on top of their learning without needing to email or message admin.

Client Dashboard

Corporate HR contacts see their employees' progress — attendance rates, grades, and teacher feedback — and can download branded PDF reports for internal stakeholders.

Zoom integration: When teachers start a Zoom class, the system automatically tracks which students joined, how long they stayed, and marks attendance accordingly. No more manual timesheets.
