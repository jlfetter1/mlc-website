About MLC · Since 2003

The language training partner Mexico's most demanding organizations actually keep.

Founded in 2003. 100+ corporate clients. 8 languages. The quiet standard for executive language training in Mexico — trusted by BMW, PepsiCo, Grupo Salinas, Médica Sur, and the teams that can't afford a miss.

Why MLC exists

The gap we were built to fill.

In 2003, Mexico's corporate English training market was full of either chain schools (high volume, low rigor) or high-end tutors (boutique, unscalable, no reporting). Neither fit a modern HR function. MLC was built to fill that gap: executive-grade rigor at corporate scale, with the reporting infrastructure HR needs to justify the spend. Twenty years later, that's still what we do.

What we believe

Three pillars.

01

Live instruction beats software.

Corporate language training is a human problem. Software drills grammar; it can't teach an executive how to lose a negotiation gracefully or hold a room through a Q&A.

02

Content must fit the work.

Generic lessons about vacations don't teach your team to negotiate a supply contract. Our AI content engine adapts every session to your sector.

03

Measure everything you promise.

If we say a program delivers, we deliver numbers. CEFR progression. Attendance. Cost-per-student. Training without measurement is faith-based spending. We don't do faith.
