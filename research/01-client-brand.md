## Key Observations & Gaps
1. **No social proof on homepage** — No testimonials, client logos, or case studies visible
2. **Weak SEO metadata** — Title is a long sentence, no clear meta description strategy
3. **No blog/content marketing** — No visible blog section or thought leadership
4. **Bilingual complexity** — Full dual-language site adds maintenance burden
5. **CTA clarity** — "Lo quiero ¡Cotizar ya!" is enthusiastic but could be more specific
6. **No pricing visible** — Everything routes to contact forms (common in B2B)
7. **WordPress + Elementor** — Heavy page builder stack, likely slow performance
8. **Cloudflare Turnstile** — Using bot protection on forms
